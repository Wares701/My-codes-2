My journey began with learning the basics of HTML. The power of web development fascinated me from day one. I spent countless hours practicing and building projects, advancing from simple static pages to complex web applications.

The field of development is constantly evolving, with new frameworks and tools being released regularly. That's why I believe in the power of Xn learning, where X represents the hours invested, and n represents the consistency factor.

Currently, I'm focusing on mastering algorithms with Big O notation of O(n2) complexity and better. The journey is challenging but incredibly rewarding.
