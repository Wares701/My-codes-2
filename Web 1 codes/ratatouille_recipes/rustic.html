Rustic Country-Style Ratatouille

This hearty, rustic version of ratatouille embraces the traditional cooking methods of the French countryside. With chunky vegetables and bold flavors, this dish captures the essence of provincial cooking.

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
3 Large tomatoes, roughly chopped
2 Large onions, chunky cut
1 Large eggplant, large chunks
3 Bell peppers, rough chunks
4 Garlic cloves, crushed
1/3 cup Olive oil
2 tbsp Herbes de Provence

Cooking Instructions

  1. Initial Preparation

    Cut vegetables into large, rustic chunks. Heat olive oil in a heavy-bottomed pot.

  2. Building Flavors

    Cook onions and garlic until fragrant. Add peppers and eggplant, cooking until they start to soften.

  3. Slow Cooking

    Add tomatoes and herbs. Simmer on low heat for 1-2 hours, stirring occasionally.

  4. Finishing

    Let rest for 30 minutes to allow flavors to meld. Serve with crusty bread.
