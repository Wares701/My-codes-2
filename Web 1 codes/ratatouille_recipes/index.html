Classic Ratatouille Recipe

Discover the authentic taste of Provence with our traditional ratatouille recipe. This colorful Mediterranean dish combines summer vegetables in a delightful stew that's both healthy and bursting with flavor.

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
2 Large eggplants, cubed
4 Roma tomatoes, chopped
2 Zucchini, sliced
2 Bell peppers, chopped
1 Large onion, diced
4 cloves Garlic, minced

Cooking Instructions

  1. Prepare the Vegetables

    Cut all vegetables into uniform sizes. Salt the eggplant and let it drain for 30 minutes.

  2. Initial Cooking

    Saut√© onions and garlic until translucent. Add bell peppers and cook until slightly softened.

  3. Layer and Simmer

    Add remaining vegetables, herbs, and simmer covered for 45 minutes until vegetables are tender.

  4. Final Touch

    Season with salt and pepper, drizzle with olive oil, and garnish with fresh basil.
