Modern Ratatouille (Confit Byaldi)

Inspired by the famous movie "Ratatouille," this elegant version of the classic dish features thinly sliced vegetables arranged in a stunning spiral pattern. This contemporary interpretation maintains the traditional flavors while creating a visually striking presentation.

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
2 Japanese eggplants, thinly sliced
6 Roma tomatoes, thinly sliced
2 Yellow squash, thinly sliced
2 Zucchini, thinly sliced
1 cup Tomato sauce (piperade)
1/4 cup Extra virgin olive oil
2 sprigs Fresh thyme

Cooking Instructions

  1. Prepare the Base

    Spread the tomato sauce (piperade) evenly in the bottom of a round baking dish.

  2. Arrange Vegetables

    Layer the sliced vegetables in an alternating pattern from the outer edge to the center of the dish.

  3. Season and Bake

    Drizzle with olive oil, add thyme, and cover with parchment paper. Bake at 375Â°F for 45-50 minutes.

  4. Final Presentation

    Remove the parchment, bake for an additional 10 minutes until vegetables are tender and lightly browned.
