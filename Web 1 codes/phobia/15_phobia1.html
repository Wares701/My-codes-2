Understanding Thalassophobia

Thalassophobia is an intense, irrational fear of the sea or large bodies of water. People with this phobia may feel anxious when near the ocean or even when they think about vast, open water.

Symptoms and Impact

Individuals with thalassophobia often experience physical and emotional symptoms when they encounter the ocean or large bodies of water

Treatment and Coping Strategies

Treatment for thalassophobia often involves a combination of therapies such as cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT) and exposure therapy.

  • Shortness of breath
  • Rapid Breathing
