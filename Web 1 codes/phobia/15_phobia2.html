Understanding Coulrophobia

Coulrophobia is an intense, irrational fear of clowns. People with this phobia may feel uneasy or terrified when they see clowns, whether in person or in media such as movies or TV shows.

Symptoms and Impact

Individuals with coulrophobia may experience a range of physical and emotional symptoms when exposed to clowns.

Treatment and Coping Strategies

Treatment for coulrophobia often involves a combination of therapies such as cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT) and exposure therapy.
