Understanding Nyctophobia

Nyctophobia is an intense fear of the dark or nighttime. This fear is more than just discomfortâ€”it can cause overwhelming anxiety that disrupts daily life.

Symptoms and Impact

Individuals with nyctophobia may experience physical and emotional symptoms when exposed to darkness. These include rapid heartbeat, trembling, sweating, nausea, and feelings of panic.

Treatment and Coping Strategies

Treatment for nyctophobia typically includes cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT) and exposure therapy.
